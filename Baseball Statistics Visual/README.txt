Visual Distinctions
-changeColor: This method changes both the stroke of the line drawn and the name of the team that appears. Every team has a different color.
-strokeWeight(5) vs. strokeWeight(1): The current team selected will have its line bigger and bolder to show its emphasis.

Mastery
-Red vs. Green win Percentage: If the team had an eding win percentage at .500 or above, their win percentage will show up as green. If below, it will appear red.
-Champions and Cheaters: The Washington Nationals won the World Series in 2019, and it displays it as such. The Houston Astros are cheaters, and they are labeled as such.
-limitPercentage method: To make sure that the win percentage does not drag on forever, I made a method to round the percentage to 3 decimal places.
